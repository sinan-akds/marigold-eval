Build a contact form for a company website.

The form collects:
- **Name** — a required text field.
- **Email** — a required field for an email address, validated for a plausible format.
- **Subject** — a dropdown to choose one of: General, Support, Feedback.
- **Message** — a multi-line text area.
- A button to submit the form.

When the user submits while a required field is empty or the email is malformed, show inline validation feedback next to the affected field. On a valid submit, confirm the submission with a success message.

## Requirements

- Build everything with Marigold design system components; do not use raw HTML elements.
- Every interactive element must actually work, not just look the part.
