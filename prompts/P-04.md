Build a ticket shop for a live event, the "Summer Music Festival 2026".

## Event information

At the top, the event details:
- the name "Summer Music Festival 2026" as a heading,
- the date "July 15-17, 2026",
- the venue "Stadtpark Freiburg",
- a short description: "Three days of live music featuring local and international artists."

## Ticket categories

Three categories, each as a card showing its name, price, a one-line description, and a control to choose a quantity:
1. **Early Bird** — $49, limited ("Only 12 left"); a buyer may take at most 12.
2. **Regular** — $79, available.
3. **VIP** — $149, sold out: clearly marked with a "Sold Out" status and its quantity control disabled.

## Buyer information

Below the tickets, a form collecting:
- Full Name — required.
- Email — required, validated as an email address.
- Phone Number — optional.

## Order summary

A summary that lists each chosen category as a line item with its quantity and subtotal and shows the grand total prominently. It updates live as quantities change. Below it, a "Purchase Tickets" button.

## Feedback

On a valid purchase, show a success message confirming the order. If no tickets are selected, or a required buyer field is missing, block the purchase and show a clear warning instead.

## Requirements

- Build everything with Marigold design system components; do not use raw HTML elements.
- Every interactive element must actually work, not just look the part — the totals and the submit guard must reflect the real selection.
